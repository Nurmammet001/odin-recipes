Lasagna

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta.

Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, sucf as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.
